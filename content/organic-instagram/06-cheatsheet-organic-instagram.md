# Cheatsheet: Organic Instagram

We trimmed our strategy section into a concise cheat sheet. You’ll find all of the tactics without the context or reasoning. Consider this a checklist for you to reference while you implement your Instagram strategy. 

## How to get started with Organic Instagram

### Here's a simple framework to help you reach customer-content fit:

- Decide what your narrative is
- Create 3-5 content buckets that support the broader narrative
- Refuse to post anything that does not fall into one of those buckets
- Consistently post that content
- Post more of what resonates, less of what doesn’t

### Research your space

- Learn what types of content drive the most organic engagement and reach.
- Discover adjacent brands who can help you access new audiences to accelerate growth.
- Use brands you know to discover adjacent brands you don’t know. 
- Study their content and bookmark them for partnership opportunities.
- Search for relevant hashtags to learn what top-performing content looks like.

### Verify user engagement

- Weed out accounts with fake followings.
- Use a tool like Modash to help.

### Understanding the algorithm

- Understand the three main ranking signals: 
- Interest — Instagram uses past behavior to "predict" which content you'll enjoy seeing and engaging with in the future.
- Recency — Recent posts are prioritized above older posts.
- Relationship — Instagram prioritizes content from accounts you interact with frequently. 
- Instagram rewards accounts who post consistently high-quality content that drives authentic engagement.

## The optimal posting strategy

### Step 1: Post content that people engage with

- Feed posts
- Stories
- Highlights
- Reels
- IG TV
- IG Live
- Guides

### Step 2: Increase reach and get followers

Key strategies:

- Get others to share your content with their audiences
- Get discovered on hashtag pages and the Explore feed
- Seek out organic partnerships and collaborations

#### Optimize your profile for SEO and follows

People who visit your profile page are more inclined to follow you when:

- They understand your value props without having to think too much.
- They’re able to self-identify with a distinct theme that’s relevant to their interests.
- They intuit that your profile is real, active, and focused on the audience rather than itself.
Elements of a high-converting profile: 

- Username — Include your company name at the front of your username for SEO.
- Name and description —Include relevant industry keywords in the name field for searchability.
- Add a CTA —Include a CTA that brings followers into your sales funnel or sends them to a product page (could be a link or DM invitation).
- Highlights — Showcase your best stories (results, valuable tips, PR).
- Home feed — For evergreen content that accurately portrays your brand and specific value you provide. Posts should complement each other to create a cohesive grid and aesthetic

#### Interact with brands and personal accounts

Plan to spend at least 30 minutes a day interacting with other brand accounts and their followers:

- Make sure to like and reply to comments on your brand page. 
- Keep tabs on adjacent brands. 
- Engage with Stories and Reels. 
- Like and comment on their followers’ posts. 

#### Seek out organic partnerships

- Brand-by-brand partnerships are one of the only ways to increase organic reach without any monetary spend. 
- The goal is to absorb followers from large accounts with similar audiences.
- Two ways to do this: Product for post and share-for-share (S4S)

### Step 3: Strategies to convert followers into leads and customers

#### Use the swipe-up feature

- If you have 10k or more followers, you can use the swipe up feature in your stories to send traffic to a link of your choosing.
- Give followers valuable insights through “Story storms.” (credit to [Ross Simmonds](https://twitter.com/TheCoolestCool) for this term)
- Invite them to engage, ask for feedback, and lead them to a relevant offer.

#### Drive traffic to a link in your bio

Test different offers:

- Shop pages
- New posts
- Lead magnets
- Download in the app store
- Use a Linktree link

#### Tag products in shoppable posts

- Tag shoppable products featured in your photos or Reels videos.
- Set up an Instagram Shop page
- Sign up for Instagram Checkout
