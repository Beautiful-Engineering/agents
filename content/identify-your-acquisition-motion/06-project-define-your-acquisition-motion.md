# Project: Define your acquisition motion

Your job right now isn’t to pick tactics or channels. That comes later. For now, your job is to **identify which acquisition motions are viable for your business.**

### **Step 1 — Review the four motion types**

- **Paid-led**
- **Content-led**
- **Virality-led**
- **Sales-led**

### **Step 2 — Decide which ones are potentially viable**

Use your **Foundational Five** and **guardrails** as filters. For example:

- If your ARPU is very low, a Sales-led motion isn’t viable. At least not as a long-term option.
- If you have a high ARPU, all of the motions may be viable from a model-channel fit perspective. Your Guardrails, product-channel, and the four motion alignment factors should be used to hone in from there.
Don’t worry about sequencing or prioritization yet — just identify the set of motions that could realistically fit.

Here’s a **quick evaluation checklist to get you started:**

- **ARPU & Payback:** Does your ARPU and payback window support the CAC band of this motion?
- **Time constraints:** Do you need signal in weeks (Paid / Sales) or can you invest months (Content / Virality) without seeing meaningful results?
- And remember, you decide what “meaningful” means.

- **Audience behavior:** Do they seek educational content? Are they guarded (marketers and engineers, for example, tend to put walls up via ad blockers as well as being wary of cold outreach).
- **Team fit:** Do you have the skills and capacity (creative, content, sales ops) this motion demands?
- **Foundation fit:** Does the motion align with the rest of your Foundational Five?

### **Step 3 — Record in your **[**Master Strategy Doc**](https://demandcurve.notion.site/Master-Strategy-Project-256f852211328013897fc2aa816684c6?source=copy_link)

Go to the **Growth Motions** section of your doc and:

- Enter the acquisition motions you believe are viable now vs down the road.
- Add any quick notes on *why* each one is or is not viable.
- Provide your assessment as to how your motions align with the rest of your Growth System.
- Call out any risks/unknowns that would need to be validated to prove out the motions.
Later, in **Part 5: Acquisition Strategy**, you’ll learn how to:

- Narrow to your **primary acquisition motion**
- Map your motion into **specific channels and tactics**
- Prioritize sequencing between **flywheels and linear channels**
For now: identify the viable set. That’s your foundation.
