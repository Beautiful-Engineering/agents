# Project: Tap your network

## Project Instructions:

- Visit and copy [this template](https://demandcurve.notion.site/Tap-Your-Network-Project-253f8522113280e2af88c287f0cb450f?source=copy_link)
- Go through every contact you might have (LinkedIn, email, phone, social, etc.) that you feel might be relevant to your product and add them to the database below.
- Mark down how you met and any other relevant information about the contact.
- Use the 3-part priority scoring system. Only contact those who score a 6 or above. (All 6+ contacts filter into the “High Priority” view.)
- Determine the primary goal of contacting them. Are they a potential first customer? Great candidate for product feedback? Mark that down.
- Open each contact and add the “Network Contact” template to the page. Follow the instructions in that template for research and email writing.
- After you send the initial emails, add a follow-up date for 2 days after the initial email was sent. In the “Needs Follow Up” view of the database, you can keep track of who needs to be followed up with.
