# Intro to monetization strategy

You’ve already defined your **monetization motion.** Whether your company is sales-led, product-led, or a hybrid motion. That gave you the high-level structure of how you’ll turn users into revenue.

Now it’s time to take the next step: designing your **monetization and pricing strategy**. This strategy translates your chosen motion into concrete decisions about *what you’ll charge for, how and when you’ll charge, and how much you’ll charge*.

By the end of this unit, you’ll have a clear plan for how your company will generate revenue in a way that’s aligned with your guardrails, your growth foundation, and your overall growth engine design.

**What you’ll learn:**

- How to design a monetization and pricing strategy that supports your growth engine
- The three components of a monetization strategy.
- How to synthesize these choices into a clear, actionable strategy
