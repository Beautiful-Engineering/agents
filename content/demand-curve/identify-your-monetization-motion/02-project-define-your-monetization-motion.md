# Project: Define Your Monetization Motion

Log your decision in the [**Master Strategy Project**](https://demandcurve.notion.site/Master-Strategy-Project-256f852211328013897fc2aa816684c6?source=copy_link)** → Growth Motions** section. For monetization, capture:

- **Motion choice**: Product-led, Sales-led, or Hybrid.
- **Viability**: Mark as *Viable Now* or *Viable Later*.
- **Analysis of viability**: Why is it viable or not yet? (e.g. ARPU levels, customer buying patterns, ability to self-serve vs. need for reps).
- **System alignment**: How well does this motion align with your:
- **Guardrails** (target CAC, payback window)
- **Foundational Five** (esp. model + market fits)
- **Alignment factors** (velocity, commitment, cost)
- **Story System** (how your monetization motion reinforces your positioning and buyer narrative)

#### When put together, you should have a short strategic narrative like:

*We are pursuing a ****product-led monetization**** motion. This motion is ****viable now**** because [strategic analysis]. It aligns with our [guardrails, foundation, alignment factors, story system] in the following way [insert reasoning].*
