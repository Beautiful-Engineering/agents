# Creator Briefs

### **Brief them without micromanaging**

**Here’s an example of a real creator brief that goes out to clients:**

## **UGC Creator Brief: (Company) “Day in the Life” Video**

### **Brand Overview**

(Company) is a Brooklyn-based coworking community built for people who’ve outgrown working from home or cafés. With **seven neighborhood locations**, (Company) provides a quiet, design-forward space to focus, complete with all the amenities: coffee, tea, snacks, phone booths, conference rooms, and transparent pricing.

### **Project Goal**

We want to create an authentic, aspirational **Day in the Life** video that shows what it’s like to work at (Company). The video should **inspire viewers to imagine themselves working here**, making them want to book a tour and join the community.

### **Concept**

- **Style:** First-person, casual vlog/UGC style.
- **Storyline:** Walk us through your day using (Company) as your workspace.
- Morning setup/ routine: leaving home, walking through the neighborhood.
- Arriving at (Company) (show exterior sign/storefront).
- Setting up at your desk.
- Grabbing coffee/tea, a quick snack.
- Taking a call in a phone booth.
- Showing one of the outdoor patios.
- Wrapping up and going home

- Doing something cool after work that reinforces the persona of someone who works at (Company) (Gym, going for a run, going out for drinks with friends etc).

### **Tone & Feel**

- **Aspirational but relatable**: make it look like the kind of place people *want* to work.
- **Cool, neighborhood vibe**: think “I upgraded from the café, but it still feels local and approachable.”
- **Energetic yet calm**: highlight productivity and flow without making it feel stiff or corporate.

### **Key Messages to Weave In**

- (Company) is **your neighborhood coworking space**.
- It’s the **perfect upgrade** if you’ve outgrown working from home or cafés.
- **Amenities included**: coffee, tea, snacks, phone booths, meeting rooms, outdoor patios.
- **Transparent pricing** and **flexible memberships** with no hidden fees.
- The **community is local and friendly**, no big corporate vibe.

### **Deliverables**

- 1 x 20–30 second video (TikTok/Reels style).
- Vertical format (9:16).
- Include **on-screen captions** for accessibility.

### **Call to Action**

End with a clear invitation to:

“Book a tour at (Company) today.”

### **Payment timing and logistics**

**For paid collaborations:**

- **50% upfront, 50% after posting** (for new creators you're testing)
- **100% within 48 hours of posting** (for creators you've worked with before)
- **Net 30 payment terms** (if you need cash flow flexibility, but communicate this upfront)
**Payment methods:**

- PayPal, Venmo, Stripe or bank transfer (ask their preference)
- For international creators: Wise (formerly TransferWise) or PayPal
**Payment communication:**

Your content just went live and looks amazing!

Payment of $X will be sent via [method] within [timeframe]. I'll send you the confirmation once it's processed.

Thanks again for the great work!
