# Project: Define Your Retention Motion

Log your decision in the [**Master Strategy Project**](https://demandcurve.notion.site/Master-Strategy-Project-256f852211328013897fc2aa816684c6?source=copy_link)** → Growth Motions** section. For retention, capture:

- **Motion choice**: Product-led, Sales-led, or Hybrid.
- **Viability**: Mark as *Viable Now* or *Viable Later*.
- **Analysis of viability**: Why is it viable or not yet? (e.g. product complexity, ARPU, customer expectations, current team capacity).
- **System alignment**: How well does this motion align with your:
- **Guardrails** (CAC, ARPU, payback, budget)
- **Foundational Five** (esp. product + market fits)
- **Alignment factors** (velocity, commitment, cost)
- **Story System** (how your retention motion reinforces your core story and customer journey)

#### When put together, you should have a short strategic narrative like:

“We are pursuing a **product-led retention** motion. This motion is **Viable Now** because [strategic analysis]. It aligns with our [guardrails, foundation, alignment factors, story system] in the following way [insert reasoning].
