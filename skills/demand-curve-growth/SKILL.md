# Demand Curve Growth System

Quick reference for growth strategy frameworks. These files distill the structured question sequences, scoring rubrics, and decision templates from the full Demand Curve curriculum (404 lessons, 53 courses).

## When to Use

Use these frameworks when:
- Building a growth strategy for a new product
- Evaluating product-market fit and foundational alignment
- Designing acquisition/monetization/retention engines
- Writing copy, landing pages, or ad creatives
- Choosing and prioritizing growth channels
