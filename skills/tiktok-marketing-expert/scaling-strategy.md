# Scaling Strategy

Multi-account growth, automation timing, and cross-platform expansion. Only read this when the user has hit scaling prerequisites — premature scaling wastes effort.

## Scaling Prerequisites

**Do NOT scale until ALL of these are true:**
- ✓ Posted consistently for 30+ days
- ✓ At least one video with 100K+ views (or multiple with 10K+)
- ✓ Found 2-3 content formats that reliably get views
- ✓ Actually getting downloads/customers from content

> Scaling 0 views × 10 accounts = still 0 views. Get it working on one account first.

---

## The Scaling Math

| Stage | Setup | Daily Output | Expected Results |
|-------|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Start** | 1 account, 1 post/day | 1 post | ~100 downloads/day |
| **Growth** | 2 accounts, 3 posts/day each | 6 posts | ~600 downloads/day |
| **Scale** | 4 accounts across TikTok + IG | 12+ posts | 30,000+ downloads in 30 days |

The math is simple: more accounts × more posts × proven formats = more reach. But each account must be established properly or you're multiplying by zero.

---

## 3-Phase Scaling Timeline

### Phase 1: Increase Volume on Main Account (Days 30-45)
- Go from 1 → 2-3 posts/day
- Space posts 3-4 hours apart
- Mix proven formats with trend posts (80/20)
- Still posting manually — stay close to what's working
- Goal: validate that higher volume doesn't tank engagement

### Phase 2: Add a Second Account (Days 45-60)
- Create account #2 on the same platform
- **Establish presence first** — follow the Phase 2 warmup protocol (7 days of engagement before posting)
- Post your proven content on the new account
- Now you're at 4-6 posts/day across 2 accounts
- Goal: prove the formats work on a fresh account

### Phase 3: Expand to Other Platforms (Days 60+)
- Take winning TikTok videos → Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Twitter
- What didn't work on TikTok might blow up on Instagram (different audiences)
- Different platforms = different audiences = more total reach
- At this point you're managing 10+ posts/day — manual posting becomes unsustainable
- Goal: maximize total reach across platforms

## Scaling Rules

### Account Establishment (Non-Negotiable)
- **ALWAYS** establish presence on new accounts before posting (7-day warmup)
- **ALWAYS** post manually from the mobile app for the first 10 posts per account
- **NEVER** use automation on accounts getting less than 500 views/video
- **NEVER** post the exact same video on the same platform twice (unless you change metadata/sound or wait many months)

### Content Quality at Scale
- Don't sacrifice quality for volume — 3 good posts beat 10 bad ones
- Each post should still follow your proven format and hook formula
- Batch-generate content but review each piece before posting
- Keep tracking performance — what works on account 1 may differ on account 2

## When to Automate

### The Automation Threshold
Automation (PostBridge, carousel CLI) is appropriate when:
1. You've posted at least **10 manual posts** on the account
2. You're **consistently getting 500+ views** per video
3. You have **proven formats** that reliably get engagement
4. Managing posts manually is becoming a bottleneck (10+ posts/day)

### What to Automate
| Tool | When to Use | What It Does |
|------|------------|-------------|
| **Warmup Bot** | New account creation | Automates Phase 2 community building — but you should still manually note formats and trends |
| **Carousel CLI** | After finding winning slideshow format | Batch-generates AI slideshows with text overlays |
| **PostBridge** | After 10+ manual posts per account | Upload once → post to all platforms, schedule a week of content in one sitting |

### What NOT to Automate
- Format discovery (Phase 3) — this requires human creativity and judgment
- Trend identification — requires being in-the-moment on the platform
- Comment replies — early engagement needs a human touch
- Music selection — trending sounds change constantly, human adds the finishing touch

### The Draft Workflow (Recommended)
Even with automation, post as drafts first and let a human:
1. Pick trending sound/music
2. Review caption one last time
3. Hit publish

This takes ~60 seconds per post. Music is everything on TikTok and can't be added via API. Trending sounds change constantly.

## Cross-Platform Expansion

### Platform Priority Order
1. **TikTok** — highest organic reach for new accounts, algorithm gives new content a fair shot
2. **Instagram Reels** — massive audience, good for brands, supports bio links
3. **YouTube Shorts** — growing fast, longer shelf life than TikTok
4. **Twitter/X** — video getting more push, different audience demographic

### Cross-Posting Rules
- Same video content works across platforms (adjust aspect ratio if needed)
- **Remove watermarks** — TikTok watermark on Instagram Reels gets suppressed
- Captions may need slight tweaks per platform (hashtag culture differs)
- Posting times vary by platform — test and optimize
- What bombs on one platform may go viral on another — don't assume

### Platform-Specific Notes
- **Instagram:** Creator/Business account for bio link. Trial Reels available to test without algorithm risk
- **TikTok:** Algorithm gives new content fair chance. Slideshows getting 2.9x more engagement. Music is critical
- **YouTube Shorts:** Longer shelf life, content can resurface weeks later. Good for evergreen formats
- **Twitter/X:** Short clips and carousel images. Algorithm pushing video content. More text-driven audience
