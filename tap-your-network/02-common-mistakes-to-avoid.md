# Common mistakes to avoid

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/5ca4f46f1600f04b73b1ccfe/68d9d224e65e3ad1c0e1e366_image.avif)

### The Broadcast Approach

Posting "I just launched! Please check it out!" on social media is the equivalent of shouting into the void. Zero personalization = zero results. Plus, it makes it very easy to ignore since people can think, “if hundreds of other people saw the post, surely someone else will help.”

### Generic Email Blast

Sending the same email to 100 people feels efficient but comes across as spam. People can tell.

### Leading with Big Asks

"Can you share this with your 10,000 followers?" is a huge ask. Start with "Would you try this and tell me what you think?"

### The Guilt Trip

"I'd really appreciate your support" puts pressure on relationships. Instead, focus on value: "I think this could really help with [their problem]."

### Forgetting to Follow Up

Most people are busy, not ignoring you. A polite follow-up often gets results.
