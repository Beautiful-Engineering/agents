## Key Rewrites

### Opening / Hook
**Before**: "I'm doing a new experiment. I'm build an AI course where I'll be distilling all the things I've been learning throughout the past years in tinkering and building with AI."
**After**: "The most valuable skill of the next decade won't be coding. / It'll be knowing how to build things. / Those sound the same. They're not."
**Why**: The original opened with "I" and gave no reason to click "see more." The rewrite opens with a bold, debatable claim that creates a curiosity gap and forces the click.

### Transformation Story
**Before**: "Now, with AI tools, and specially with the latest models from December, I feel like I have instantly gained superpowers."
**After**: "I described what I wanted. An AI agent built it. I shipped a product with auth, payments, and a real database -- in a weekend. No code written by me."
**Why**: "Gained superpowers" is abstract and unfalsifiable. The rewrite shows a specific, visual, concrete result the reader can picture.

### Macro Observations
**Before**: "But I also see how we're about to face (if not already) a new way of doing work, a new way of interacting with technology and basically a radically different world."
**After**: Cut entirely. The macro predictions were the weakest part of the original -- vague, generic, and not unique to the author. Replaced with the specific coding vs. building distinction, which is a unique insight.
**Why**: Macro AI predictions are noise on LinkedIn. Unique insights are signal.

### Course Details
**Before**: One dense paragraph mixing format details, content overview, and caveats.
**After**: Clean bullet list of what's inside, followed by a separate short paragraph for logistics (async, WIP, free Module 0).
**Why**: LinkedIn readers scan. Bullets are scannable. Dense paragraphs are not.

### CTA / Close
**Before**: "You can find more information at [URL]" buried mid-paragraph, followed by a disconnected closing line.
**After**: "Builder generalists. That's the bet." followed by the URL on its own line.
**Why**: The close now ties back to the opening thesis, gives the reader an identity to adopt, and ends with confidence. The URL stands alone so it's impossible to miss.
