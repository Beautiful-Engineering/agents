## The System Produces Your Growth Strategy

Many of you joined this program with one primary goal: **to build a growth strategy.**

And I have good news. That’s exactly what you’re doing here.

Before we dive into the first layer of the Growth System, let’s clarify what we mean by strategy and how our approach works. These days, most of my time is spent thinking about, discussing, and developing growth strategies.

And if there’s one thing I can confidently say, it’s that strategy is one of the most misunderstood concepts in the startup world. On one end, I see teams that put together goals and roadmaps and declare, “Voilà, strategy!” On the other, I see teams who treat it like some mystical exercise, trying to peer into the future and map out every move for the next decade.

In reality, strategy is far more straightforward. 

*Good strategy means identifying the right problem, diagnosing its root causes, setting the boundaries you have to work within, and defining the rules of the game that inform how and what you’ll do to solve it.*
