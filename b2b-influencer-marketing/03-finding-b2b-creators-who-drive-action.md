# Finding B2B Creators Who Drive Action

### **Green Flags of Purchase-Driving Creators**

There's a massive difference between creators with big audiences and creators whose audiences actually buy things.

**Comments show buying intent:**

- "Where did you get this?"
- "Just ordered, thanks for the rec!"
- "Link in your bio?"
- "Is this worth the price?"
**Audience asks for advice:**

- "Which option would you choose?"
- "Have you tried alternatives?"
- "Does this actually work?"
**Followers discuss implementation:**

- "I tried this and here's what happened..."
- "This worked for me because..."
- "Here's how I'm using it..."
**Price/value conversations happen naturally:**

- "Worth the investment?"
- "Cheaper alternative?"
- "ROI has been amazing"

### **Red Flags of Passive Audiences**

- **Only emoji engagement:** Fire emojis don't buy products
- **Generic compliments:** "Love this!" without specifics shows surface-level engagement
- **High follower-to-comment ratio mismatch:** 50K followers but 15 comments per post
- **No follow-up questions:** Audiences that don't ask questions don't take action
- **Sudden follower spikes** without corresponding content quality improvement
- **Comments from accounts** with no profile photos or post history

### **Your Creator Discovery Sprint**

Don't spend weeks researching. Spend 2-3 focused hours and get started.

### **Hour 1: Hashtag archaeology**

Search platform-specific hashtags your target buyers actually follow on **LinkedIn, YouTube, or X (Twitter)**. For a SaaS or B2B tool, this might look like:

- **For productivity & workflow tools:** #nocode #automation #operations #projectmanagement
- **For sales tools:** #salesenablement #revops #b2bsales #crmstrategy
- **For HR/people platforms:** #futureofwork #hrtech #peopleanalytics #remotework
Look for creators who consistently share playbooks, frameworks, or real tool breakdowns—not just one-off mentions. Prioritize voices who review, demonstrate, or teach workflows.

### **Hour 2: Analyze competitor tags**

Go to similar brands' Instagram profiles → "Tagged" tab → Look for creator posts with high engagement.

Find posts where people ask for product recommendations in your category. Look at who responds with specific, detailed suggestions. These creators often have audiences that follow their advice.

**Search terms like:**

- "Best [your category] for..."
- "Anyone tried [competitor]?"
- "Looking for recommendations for..."

### **Hour 3: Reverse-engineer your users**

Start with your ideal target market, then analyze who they follow to find creators they already resonate with. This reverse-engineering approach ensures you're finding influencers your potential customers actually trust and engage with, rather than just popular accounts in your general space.

Ask your best current users: "Which creators do you follow for [relevant topic]?" This often reveals perfect audience matches that traditional influencer discovery tools miss.

**Your goal:** Find 10 creators with:

- 5K-50K followers (the sweet spot for engagement and affordability)
- 3-5% engagement rate on recent posts
- Comments that show genuine audience connection, not just emoji reactions
- Audience demographics that match your target users
End with 20-30 creators who show signs of actually driving action, not just awareness.

**Why this approach works:** You're not guessing about audience fit—you're following the digital breadcrumbs of people who already match your ideal customer profile. The creators they follow are proven to influence their decisions and capture their attention.

### The Creator Evaluation Framework

Before reaching out, evaluate each creator across three dimensions:

### Audience Alignment (Most Critical)

**Demographics:** Age, location, gender**Psychographics:** Values, interests, lifestyle align with your brand**Behavioral:** Audience actually follows recommendations and makes purchases

**Quick test:** Read through 20-30 comments on their recent posts. Do these sound like people who would use your product?

### Engagement Quality

**Calculate true engagement:** (Comments + saves + shares) ÷ followers**Target:** 3-5% for creators with 5K-50K followers**Look for:** Meaningful conversations, not just reactions
