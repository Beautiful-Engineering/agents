# Essential Tracking & Legal Setup

### **Essential tracking setup (do this first)**

Before any outreach, set up tracking infrastructure:

### **UTM parameters for each creator**

- yoursite.com?utm_source=instagram&utm_campaign=creatorname
- Track in Google Analytics under Acquisition > Campaigns

### **Unique discount codes**

- Format: CREATORNAME15 (creator name + discount percentage)
- Set up in your checkout system with individual tracking

### **Creator-specific landing pages** (optional but powerful)

- yoursite.com/creatorname
- Personalize messaging to match their audience

### **Conversion tracking**

- Google Analytics goals or Facebook Pixel events
- Track signups, purchases, or whatever matters most

> **Time investment:** 2-3 hours upfront saves weeks of guesswork later.

### **Product readiness audit: Are you actually ready?**

Before reaching out to creators, audit whether your product can handle influencer traffic and convert it effectively.

### **Traffic readiness checklist**

- **Website can handle 5x normal traffic** without crashing
- **Mobile experience is smooth** (most creator traffic comes from mobile)
- **Loading times under 3 seconds** on mobile and desktop
- **Clear value proposition** visible within 5 seconds of landing

### **Conversion readiness checklist**

- **Onboarding flow is bulletproof** - can a confused user figure it out in 2 minutes?
- **Social proof is visible** - testimonials, user counts, or press mentions on your homepage
- **Signup process is frictionless** - minimal required fields, clear next steps
- **Email sequences are set up** to nurture new signups into active users

### **Red flag audit**

**Skip influencer marketing if:**

- Your product has major usability issues that cause user frustration
- You can't handle customer support for 50+ new users per week
- Your onboarding completion rate is below 40%
- You don't have basic analytics set up to track user behavior
**Why this matters:** Nothing kills creator relationships faster than sending traffic to a broken experience. Fix your foundation first.

### **Legal basics (keep it simple for now)**

### **FTC compliance checklist**

- **Paid partnerships:** #ad or #sponsored at the beginning of captions
- **Gifted products:** #gifted or #pr for anything with value (no minimum threshold)
- **Affiliate links:** #affiliate or #commission when using trackable links

### **Simple contract for paid work**

Basic Partnership Agreement

Deliverables:

- [X] Instagram posts by [date]
- Tag @[yourcompany] and use #[campaignhashtag]
- Include #ad disclosure
Payment:

- $[amount] within 30 days of content going live
- [Bonus structure if applicable]
Usage rights:

- We can share your content on our channels for 12 months
- You retain ownership of original content
Either party can cancel with 48-hour notice.

### **Crisis management (the basics)**

- ‍**If creator posts something off-brand:** Contact privately within 24 hours, discuss concerns, and either request clarification or terminate the partnership.**‍**
- **If you get negative comments:** Respond helpfully to legitimate complaints, ignore trolls, don't argue publicly.**‍**
- **If creator gets into controversy:** Pause active partnerships immediately, assess severity, make decisions based on brand alignment.
