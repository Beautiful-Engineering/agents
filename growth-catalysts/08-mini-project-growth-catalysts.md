## Mini-Project: Current & Future Catalysts

Open your [Master Strategy Project](https://demandcurve.notion.site/Master-Strategy-Project-256f852211328013897fc2aa816684c6?source=copy_link) and go to the **Growth Catalysts** section. You’ll see a simple table broken down by the six categories: Market, Product, Model, Channel, Brand, and Company.

Your job is to start filling it out in two passes:

- **Current Catalysts** – Add any catalysts you believe exist in your business today.
- **Future Catalysts** – Add any catalysts you think you could intentionally build over time. 

- *And provide your thought process in the “How could the catalyst be unlocked?” column to begin fleshing out how your foundation could be modified to unlock the respective “future” catalysts you’ve identified.
