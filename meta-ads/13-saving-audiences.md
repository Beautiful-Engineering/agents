# Saving Audiences

To save yourself time, close the window, pop open your OLD campaign, and save the audience for the best-performing ad set.

- To do that: click “Edit” under your OLD campaign’s ad set:

![Save Audiences 1](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/5ca4f46f1600f04b73b1ccfe/6837ec45d079ec18a92a7a7a_1632329533601.avif)

        2. Scroll all the way down to the bottom of the “Audience” section. Hit “Save This Audience” and name it.

![Saving Audiences 2](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/5ca4f46f1600f04b73b1ccfe/6837ec45dcaaae5738f031dd_1632329533323.avif)

       3. Now, the next time you create an ad set, you can select the saved audience you just created.

![Saving Audiences 3](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/5ca4f46f1600f04b73b1ccfe/6837ec45cc391ff85d994bc9_1632329533079.avif)
